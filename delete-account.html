MillionsTicket Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your MillionsTicket account and associated personal data at any time. Once the request is verified, we remove the account credentials, profile information, ticket history, and notification preferences from our systems.

How to submit a deletion request

  1. Send an email to millionsticket.sales@gmail.com using the subject line “MillionsTicket Account Deletion”.
  2. Include the following details so we can verify ownership:
    • Full name associated with the account.
    • Email address or phone number used to sign in.
    • Optional: brief context about the data you would like us to delete.
  3. We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days, unless additional verification is required.

What we delete

  • Authentication records (email and login metadata).
  • Profile details stored in MillionsTicket (name, reseller info, preferences).
  • Ticket scans, draw history, and locally stored analytics linked to your account.
  • Push notification tokens tied to your identity.

What we may retain

Certain aggregated or anonymized metrics that do not identify you may be retained for fraud prevention and regulatory reporting. Transaction data required by applicable laws (for example, financial or tax records) is retained for the legally mandated period and is isolated from active systems.

Need help?

If you have questions about the process or need to update your request, contact us at millionsticket.sales@gmail.com.
